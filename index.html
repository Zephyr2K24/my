About

Welcome to Zephyr, the collaborative symposium presented by the Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Information Technology (IT), and Computer Science and Business Systems (CSBS) departments of Madha Engineering College! Embark on a journey of technological innovation and academic excellence as we unite to explore the latest trends and advancements in the field. Zephyr aims to create a vibrant platform for students, faculty, and industry professionals to come together and exchange knowledge, ideas, and experiences. At Zephyr, we believe in fostering collaboration and learning through a diverse range of activities. From stimulating discussions and insightful workshops to competitive events and hands-on projects, there's something for everyone to engage with and learn from. Kernix serves as the central hub of Zephyr, providing participants with access to event schedules, resources, and networking opportunities. Join us as we celebrate the spirit of innovation and creativity, and together, let's shape the future of technology. Welcome aboard the Zephyr journey!
